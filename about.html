About · The V2 Brief

The brand is the navigator, never the aircraft.

V2 flies nothing and owns nothing but the routing. That's not a limitation — it's the whole proposition. Our only allegiance is to the client on the other end of the line.

Trust is our bond

From the ground to the skies, a life of effortless luxury is our client's right.

V2 Jets is a private-aviation broker founded in Boca Raton in 2015 by Steven Rosenzweig and Guy Endzweig. One came up inside the charter business; the other from a trading career, where his real edge was his network. Between them they built a house on a single rule: trust, not tricks.

As a broker, V2 arranges and routes private flying — it does not operate aircraft. That separation is deliberate. We aren't loyal to a tail number we need to keep busy; we're loyal to your trip. We place you in the right category for the route and clear it end to end.

The firm was built on three things and still runs on them: expertise, execution, and long-term relationships. That's how it grew from an idea to a $100M business without changing the rule it started with.

The growth keeps coming. In 2026 V2 named Alden Lebov and Andrew Hauptman Managing Partners, and acquired charter broker Corporate Aviation — of Bedford, Massachusetts and Fort Lauderdale, Florida — folding its advisory bench into a V2 team of roughly forty-five.
