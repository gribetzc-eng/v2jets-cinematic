On-Demand Charter

Program 01 · pay-as-you-fly

Fly where, when and how you want, in the utmost of luxury.

The pay-as-you-fly route. A personal, hand-selected charter specialist is yours around the clock — they own your flight from the first request to wheels-up.

Taxi time, fuel, and repositioning are included in your quote. There are no membership fees. You give us the trip; we give you the right aircraft category for it and clear the route.

Request a charter

Obsidian

Program 02 · jet card

Lock your hours. Lose the guesswork.

A fixed-rate jet card for flyers who want certainty over the spot market. Buy your hours at a known rate and draw them down as you fly — offered in 25- and 50-hour tiers.

The card is the simplest way to make your flying predictable: one rate, one navigator, the same bond on every leg.

Ask about Obsidian

V2 Vault

Program 03 · prepaid account

Fund it once. Fly it down.

A prepaid flight account for the client who flies often and plans ahead. Fund the Vault, then draw against it trip by trip — no quote-and-wire on every flight.

It keeps your routing cleared and ready, so the only decision left is where you're going next.

Open a Vault

Aircraft Management

Program 04 · for owners

Own the aircraft. Let us run the operation.

For owners and principals. We handle the operating detail of your aircraft and put it to work when you're not flying it — managed with the same bond of trust we give every client.

You keep the asset and the experience; we carry the load of running it well.
