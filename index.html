The V2 Brief

From the ground to the skies, a life of effortless luxury is our client's right.

V2 Jets is a private-aviation broker founded in Boca Raton in 2015 by Steven Rosenzweig and Guy Endzweig — one a veteran of the charter business, the other an entrepreneur whose real edge was his network.

They built the firm on three things: expertise, execution, and long-term relationships. We arrange and route your flying; we never operate the aircraft. That separation is the point — our only allegiance is to you.

The result is a house that grew from an idea to a $100M business without changing the rule it started with. Trust, not tricks.
